Welcome!

My art is life itself. My art is intentional and intuitive, it is based on a feeling.  I chose joy in my artworks and I stand by my choice every day. My art stands with me by standing out serving as a reminder: life is incredibly beautiful.  Art you will see here is about pure ENERGY. Vital, universal, wild ENERGY.  Art you will see here is about WOMEN- inspired by/made for.  Art you will see here is HUMAN-inspired by/made for. Tasteful, aesthetic, vibrant. An unapologetic celebration of individuality, every piece, every project is made for those  who walk the path of authenticity.

Connect with it. Choose and let yourself be chosen, it’s all about synergy after all.  Annette Golden, Art GODDESS.
